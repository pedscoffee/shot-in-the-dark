          Note Structure
          

            Use {input} for your one-liner, {templates} for matched content,
            {dropdown:template-id} to nest dropdown templates, and {static:TEXT} for fixed text.
            Format freely — bold, italic, headings, lists are all supported.
          

          

            

            

          

          

        

        

          
Example:

          
HPI: {input}

Educated On:
{templates}
